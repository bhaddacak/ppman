\chapter{Prosodic patterns}\label{chap:prospatt}

In the first launch of this manual, I left out the detailed description of each verse type of Pāli prosody because they make no sense for most readers. When I have an opportunity to revise the book, I still hesitate to add these formulas. At the final decision, I collect all of them as an appendix shown here.

All formulas are taken directly from the program's resources. So, they conform to the calculation in the program.\footnote{Some of them can be wrong by any chance. Knowledgeable readers should inform me to make them better.} An explanation of symbols used can be found in Chapter \ref{chap:prosody}. Verse types without ID are those unable to be analyzed by the program.
